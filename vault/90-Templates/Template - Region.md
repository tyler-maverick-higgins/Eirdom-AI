## Summary

One self-contained paragraph describing:

* What this region is
* Why it matters
* What makes it unique
* How it connects to the wider campaign

This section should stand alone and be understandable without reading the rest of the note.

## Geography

Describe:

* Terrain
* Climate
* Natural resources
* Landmarks
* Travel conditions
* Seasonal changes
* Natural hazards

## History

Document established historical events that shaped the region.

Focus on:

* Founding events
* Disasters
* Wars
* Oaths
* Migrations
* Cultural changes

Avoid speculation.

Rumors belong elsewhere.
